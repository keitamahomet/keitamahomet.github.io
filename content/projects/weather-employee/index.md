This project explores the relationship between weather conditions and employee activity patterns.

Using descriptive analytics techniques, the analysis investigates how different meteorological variables such as temperature, precipitation, and seasonal patterns may influence employee behavior and activity levels.

Through the use of statistical summaries and data visualization techniques in R, the project highlights how environmental factors can be analyzed to better understand operational dynamics and behavioral trends.
